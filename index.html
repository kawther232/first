Hot and sour cabbage

  • About1.5 ibs pale green or white cabbage
  • 3-4 tbs vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp Sichun peppercorn (page 39)
  • 4-6 small dried whole red chilies
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tb sugar
  • 1 tb light soy sauce
  • tbs rice vinegar
  • a few drops sesame oil

Steps

  1. Separate the cabbage leaves, stack 2-3 leaves on top of one another, and cut them into small pieces about the size of large pstage stamps.
  2. Heat the oil in a preheated work until hot, add the sichuan peppercon and whole chilies , and stir-fry for a few seconds.Add the cabbage and stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  3. Add the salt and stir-fry for another minute, then blend in the soy sauce and rice vinegar and stir-fry for another minute. Sprinkle with the sesame oil, and serve hot or cold.
